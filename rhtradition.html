About The Professor

Dr. Howell is my Rhetorical Traditions professor. His research interests include, Reagan at the Summit: Presidential Public Diplomacy and the Collapse of Soviet Communism,Reagan and the U.S.-Soviet Summits: Public Diplomacy, Humanity’s Dignity, and Soviet Communism’s Depravity, and Margaret Thatcher. Dr. Howell has published work in the book “Reagan and Reykjavík: Arms Control, SDI, and the Argument from Human Rights”.Dr. Howell’s teaching interests include, The Rhetorical Tradition,Communication Skills, and Introduction to Communication Studies.

About The Course

The Class is about the history of rhetoric. The course goes into detail about philosophical approaches to rhetoric as how it effects society.
