About The Professor

Dr. Evia is my Visual Media professor. Dr. Evia has contributed to the book “Outsourcing Technical Communication”. Dr. Evia’s research projects include “Science and Engineering Short Course for Technical Writing”, “Developing a Mobile User Assistance and Training Solution for Hispanic Workers“, and “Micro-processes of Latino Construction Workers' Health: Feasibility of CATS”. Evia has won the Outstanding Faculty Outreach award through the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences at Virginia Tech and is an expert in technical communication and user documentation.

About the Course

In this course we learn about how to create effective visual media. We have had 4 sections in this class and they have been magazine page layout, photography, filming videos and creating a webpage.

My Opinion

I got to learn a lot about the behind the scene action that goes into creating visual media. I know that the skills I am learning in this class will benefit me when I have a job in the real world.
