About the Professor

Dr. Cannon is my Principles of Public Relations professor. Dr. Cannon has contributed to the textbook “Study Guide for the Examination for Accreditation in Public Relations (4th edition). New York: Universal Accreditation Board”. Dr. Cannon researches “Course Development” and is a member of the Public Relations Society of America.

About The Course

Principles of Public Relations introduces students to public relations. The course is a prerequisite for higher-level public relations courses and is supposed to establish a conceptual foundation for public relations majors.

My Opinion

I don’t recommend anyone taking this course if you are not required to. Most of the content for the tests and quizzes are not discussed in class and I had to teach myself the information. The time allotted for the tests and exams is not sufficient for understanding the question and leads to poor grades.
